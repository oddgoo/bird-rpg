Welcome, oh fledgling divinity of the skies!

You have probably been invited to the world of bird-rpg, but a quick look at the #üê§-bird-rpg-actions channel felt overwhelming?

The purpose of this guide, as much as it is to explain the general idea, is to make you feel at ease. In bird-rpg, there are no mistakes, no "right way to play", no "decay mechanics", and barely any competition.

Alright, let's start small:

You have a certain amount of actions per day to invest into commands.
You can access all the commands by typing "/" in the actions channel.

But what to do first? Probably increase the size of your nest ü™µ with "/build" so you can hold more seeds. Seeds are used to lay eggs ü•ö and adopting plants üåø. Eggs hatch into birds, and birds bring bonuses to many types of actions!

üèóÔ∏è Nest Building

Commands

  • /build [amount] - Add twigs to your personal nest
  • /build_common [amount] - Add twigs to the common nest
  • /add_seed [amount] - Add seeds to your personal nest
  • /add_seed_common [amount] - Add seeds to the common nest
  • /borrow_seeds [amount] - Move seeds from common nest to your nest
  • /donate_seeds [amount] - Move seeds from your nest to common nest

Rules

  • A nest can only hold as many seeds as it has twigs
  • Moving seeds between nests doesn't count as an action
  • If no amount is specified in commands, it defaults to 1

‚ú® Customisation

Commands

  • /rename_nest [name] - Give your nest a custom name
  • /feature_bird [bird_name] - Set a bird to be featured in your nest display
  • /decorate_nest [treasure_name] [x] [y] - Decorate your nest with a treasure. The x and y parameters are optional.
  • /decorate_bird [bird_name] [treasure_name] [x] [y] - Decorate a bird with a treasure. The x and y parameters are optional.
  • /decorate_plant [plant_name] [treasure_name] [x] [y] - Decorate a plant with a treasure. The x and y parameters are optional.
  • /group [group_name] [names] - Assign birds/plants to a named group (comma-separated names). Groups display as subheadings on your profile.
  • /ungroup [group_name] - Remove all birds/plants from a named group.
  • /clean_nest - Remove all decorations from your nest.
  • /clean_bird [bird_name] - Remove all decorations from a specific bird.
  • /clean_plant [plant_name] - Remove all decorations from a specific plant.

Rules

  • You can change your nest's name as often as you like
  • You can only feature birds that you have hatched
  • Featured birds are displayed prominently in your nest

üéµ Social Interactions

Commands

  • /sing @user1 @user2 ... - Give other birds 3 extra actions for the day
  • /sing_repeat - repeat your last singing command!
  • /nests - Show status of your nest and the common nest
  • /entrust [bird_name] @user - Give one of your birds to another user
  • /regurgitate @user [amount] - Give some of your bonus actions to another user
  • /gift_treasure [treasure_name] @user - Give a foraged treasure to another user

Rules

  • Each bird can sing to another bird once per day
  • Singing helps other birds by giving them extra actions
  • You can view how many birds have sung to you in your nest status
  • You can give your birds to other users using the entrust command
  • Once a bird is entrusted, it permanently moves to the other user's nest

üíé Foraging

Commands

  • /forage [actions] - Forage for treasures in different locations
  • /cancel_forage - Cancel your ongoing foraging action

Rules

  • The more actions you invest, the faster you will find a treasure. Each location has different things to find!
  • You can only have one foraging action active at a time.
  • You can cancel your foraging action at any time to get a refund of your actions.

üçÖ Incubation & Chicks

Commands

  • /lay_egg - Convert seeds into an egg in your nest
  • /brood - Brood eggs to help them hatch (can target other players)
  • /brood_random - Brood a random nest that hasn't been brooded today
  • !brood_all - Use all remaining actions to brood eggs in all available nests
  • /bless_egg - Use 1 inspiration and 30 seeds to bless your egg. If a bird other than your most-prayed one hatches, your prayers will be preserved and a new egg will be created immediately!
  • /pray_for_bird [scientific_name] [amount] - Pray to increase a specific bird's hatching chance
  • /lock_nest - Lock your nest to prevent others from brooding your eggs üîí
  • /unlock_nest - Unlock your nest to allow others to brood your eggs üîì

Rules

  • You can only brood each egg once per day
  • You can help others by brooding their eggs
  • An egg needs to be brooded 10 times to hatch
  • Having chicks increases your daily action limit
  • Each brooding action counts as one of your daily actions
  • Praying for birds multiplies their chance of hatching from your egg
  • Each prayer consumes one action and the multiplier persists until the egg hatches
  • You can lock your nest to prevent others from brooding your eggs
  • While your nest is locked, only you can brood your own eggs

üåø Gardening

  • /plant_new [plant_name] - Adopt a new plant in your garden
  • /plant_compost [plant_name] - Give back a plant for 80% of its cost

üìî Research

Commands

  • /graduate_bird [bird_name] - Release a bird from your nest, it will boost the study action! (each released bird adds +1%)
  • /study [actions] - Invest actions into studying random authors

Rules

  • Released birds are removed from your nest but still count as discovered for the codex
  • When studying, you'll be shown a quote and must guess its author
  • If you guess correctly, your invested study points are doubled
  • Studied authors unlock bonuses for everyone based on milestones! (See the research section)

‚ú® Manifestation

Commands

  • /manifest_bird [scientific_name] [actions] - Spend actions to manifest a bird species by its scientific name
  • /manifest_plant [scientific_name] [actions] - Spend actions to manifest a plant species by its scientific name

Rules

  • You can manifest any bird or plant species that exists on iNaturalist
  • Each species requires a certain number of manifestation points based on its rarity
  • Manifestation progress is shared, and the rarity depends on their real-world observation counts
  • Once fully manifested, the species can be hatched or adopted like regular species by anyone!
  • Fully manifested species appear in the codex, and copy the effects of existing ones
  • Common species: 40 points
  • Uncommon species: 70 points
  • Rare species: 110 points
  • Mythical species: 160 points

üçÖ Pomodoro Flocks

Commands

  • /start_flock - Start a pomodoro flock session
  • /join_flock - Join another bird's flock session

Rules

  • Flock sessions last 60 minutes
  • Users have 10 minutes to join after session starts
  • Each completed session grows your garden by +1
  • Participants get 5 bonus actions for completing a session
  • Join the #video-chat channel during sessions to focus together if you wish!
