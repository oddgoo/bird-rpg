Commands

  • /build [amount] - Add twigs to your personal nest
  • /build_common [amount] - Add twigs to the common nest
  • /add_seed [amount] - Add seeds to your personal nest
  • /add_seed_common [amount] - Add seeds to the common nest
  • /borrow_seeds [amount] - Move seeds from common nest to your nest
  • /donate_seeds [amount] - Move seeds from your nest to common nest

Rules

  • A nest can only hold as many seeds as it has twigs
  • Moving seeds between nests doesn't count as an action
  • If no amount is specified in commands, it defaults to 1

‚ú® Customisation

Commands

  • /rename_nest [name] - Give your nest a custom name

Rules

  • You can change your nest's name as often as you like
  • Names must be appropriate and family-friendly

üéµ Social Interactions

Commands

  • /sing @user1 @user2 ... - Give other birds 3 extra actions for the day
  • /nests - Show status of your nest and the common nest
  • /entrust [bird_name] @user - Give one of your birds to another user

Rules

  • Each bird can sing to another bird once per day
  • Singing helps other birds by giving them extra actions
  • You can view how many birds have sung to you in your nest status
  • You can give your birds to other users using the entrust command
  • Once a bird is entrusted, it permanently moves to the other user's nest

Commands

  • /explore [region] [amount] - Explore a region to discover new birds

Rules

  • Currently available regions: Oceania
  • Exploration points are shared among all players
  • Each exploration action counts as one of your daily actions

üçÖ Incubation & Chicks

Commands

  • /lay_egg - Lay an egg in your nest (costs seeds)
  • /brood @user1 @user2 ... - Brood eggs to help them hatch (yours if no users specified)
  • /brood_random - Brood a random egg that you haven't brooded today

Rules

  • You can only brood each egg once per day
  • You can help others by brooding their eggs
  • An egg needs to be brooded 10 times to hatch
  • Having chicks increases your daily action limit
  • Each brooding action counts as one of your daily actions

üçÖ Pomodoro Flocks

Commands

  • /start_flock - Start a pomodoro flock session
  • /join_flock - Join another bird's flock session

Rules

  • Flock sessions last 60 minutes
  • Users have 10 minutes to join after session starts
  • Each completed session grows your garden by +1
  • Participants get 5 bonus actions for completing a session
  • Join the #video-chat channel during sessions to focus together!

  • Memoirs are limited to 256 characters
  • Adding a memoir grants +1 Inspiration
  • Share your bird of divinity stories and wisdom!
